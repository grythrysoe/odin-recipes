Lasagna

This is a recipe to a rich and tasty lasagna.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lbs ground beef
  • 1 jar (32 oz) tomato sauce
  • 32 oz cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzerella cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • Salt and peper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
  2. Cook ground beef in a skillet, add tomato sauce and simmer for 5 min.
  3. Combine cottage cheese, mozzerella, eggs, water, dried parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  4. Spread 3/4 cup of sauce in a baking dish and cover with 3 lasagna noodles followed by a layer of cheese mixture. Repeat 3 times and cover with aluminium foil.
  5. Bake in the oven for 45 minutes.
  6. Let stand for 10 minutes before served.
  7. Enjoy!
