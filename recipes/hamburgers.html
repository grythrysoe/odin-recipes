Hamburgers

This is a recipe to healthy and delisious hamburgers.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 8 burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Pikles
  • Big mac sauce

Directions:

  1. Combine ground beef, garlic, olive oil, mustard, and salt in a bowl and mix.
  2. Shape the mixture into 8 thin patties.
  3. Cook the patties on a pan for 3-4 minutes on each side.
  4. Assemble with buns and fillings.
  5. Enjoy!
