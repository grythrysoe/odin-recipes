Tacos

This is a recipe to crispy and delisious tacos.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lbs ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • 1 cup chunky salsa
  • 12 small corn tortillas
  • 1/2 cup white cheddar, shredded
  • 1/4 tsp salt to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Topings of choice (lettude, shredded cheese, sour cream, corn)

Directions:

  1. Cook ground beef in a skillet and add salt and taco seasoning.
  2. Preheat a large griddle over medium heat, add oil and fry tortillas for 20 seconds.
  3. Add cheese to the center of each tortilla tollowed by a cup of filling.
  4. Fold tortillas into a taco shape using a spatula.
  5. Fry the taco for 1 minutes on each side.
  6. Add toppings of choice.
  7. Enjoy!
