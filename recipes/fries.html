Homemade Fries

This is a recipe to crispy and tasty fries made from scratch.

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lbs golden yellow potatoes
  • 1 teaspon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspons garlic poweder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F.
  2. Slice potatoes into 1/4-inch-wide sticks.
  3. Drizzle potatoes with olive oil, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, and pepper.
  4. Spread potatoes into a single layer on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Flip potatoes and cook for another 5-10 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
